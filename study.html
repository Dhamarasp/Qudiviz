Video Summary

In this video, you learned essential restaurant phrases in English, including how to get the waiter's attention, order food, ask for recommendations, and request the bill.

Remember to use polite phrases like "please" and "thank you" when ordering at restaurants.

Key Phrases from Video

1

"I would like to order, please."

Use this to start ordering

2

"What do you recommend?"

Ask for suggestions

3

"Could I have the bill, please?"

Ask for the check

4

"Is service included?"

Ask about tipping
