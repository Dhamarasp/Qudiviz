Choose the correct translation for:

"I would like to order, please."

Correct!

Great job! "Me gustaría ordenar, por favor" is the correct translation.

Incorrect

The correct answer is "Me gustaría ordenar, por favor".

"Me gustaría la cuenta, por favor" means "I would like the bill, please".

Helpful Tip

In Spanish, "ordenar" means "to order" (food), and "me gustaría" is a polite way to say "I would like".
